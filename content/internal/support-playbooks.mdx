## Overview

This playbook provides step-by-step response procedures for the most common support cases. Follow these scripts to ensure consistent, compliant, and efficient customer service.

---

## Playbook 1: Failed Transfer

**Trigger:** Customer reports a transfer was deducted but not received.

### Step 1 — Gather information

Collect from the customer:
- Transaction reference (required)
- Date and time of transfer
- Amount and recipient bank

### Step 2 — Check transaction status

In admin panel: **Admin → Transactions → Search by reference**

| Status found | Action |
|-------------|--------|
| `COMPLETED` | Confirm with customer's bank, escalate if confirmed issue |
| `PENDING` | Inform customer, expected resolution time |
| `FAILED` | Check refund status → see Step 3 |
| `PROCESSING` | Monitor for 30 minutes, contact if stuck |

### Step 3 — Failed transaction refund

1. Verify refund status in the admin panel
2. If refund was not auto-processed, escalate to **#finance-ops** Slack channel
3. Inform customer: *"Your refund is being processed and will appear in your wallet within 1 hour."*

### Step 4 — Resolution SLA

| Priority | Target resolution |
|----------|-----------------|
| Failed + no refund | 2 hours |
| Failed + refund processing | 4 hours |
| Pending > 2 hours | 24 hours |

## Playbook 2: Account Access Issue

**Trigger:** Customer cannot log in or is locked out.

### Forgot password

1. Direct customer to **Login → Forgot Password**
2. If no email received after 5 minutes: check spam, verify email in admin
3. If email address changed: identity verification required before update

### Account locked

1. Check in admin: **Admin → Users → [User ID] → Security**
2. If locked due to failed attempts: inform customer it auto-unlocks after 30 minutes
3. If locked by compliance: do NOT disclose reason; escalate to **#compliance** channel

### Phone number lost

1. Collect: Full name, email, last 4 digits of BVN, government ID (uploaded to ticket)
2. Escalate to **#identity-verification** channel
3. SLA: 1–2 business days

## Playbook 3: Disputed Transaction

**Trigger:** Customer claims a transaction was unauthorised or incorrect.

### Checklist

- [ ] Transaction was made within 60 days
- [ ] Customer confirms they did not authorise it
- [ ] Collect: Reference, amount, date
- [ ] Log dispute in admin: **Admin → Disputes → New**

### Investigation steps

1. Check device and IP address of the transaction
2. Check if MFA was active at time of transaction
3. If suspicious: **escalate to fraud team immediately**
4. Suspend account access if account may be compromised

### Refund decision

| Finding | Outcome |
|---------|---------|
| Confirmed fraud | Full refund + account security reset |
| Customer authorised (forgotten) | Decline dispute, educate customer |
| Unclear | Escalate to compliance for manual review |

## Playbook 4: KYC Rejection Appeal

1. Ask customer what rejection reason they received
2. Look up submission in admin: **Admin → KYC → [User ID]**
3. Check rejection reason logged by the verification provider
4. Provide clear guidance for resubmission
5. If customer insists rejection is an error, escalate to **#kyc-review** channel
