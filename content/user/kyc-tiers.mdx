## Overview

VOPayX uses a three-tier KYC (Know Your Customer) system. Higher tiers unlock higher transaction and balance limits. Verification is fast and fully digital.

## Tier Summary

| Feature | Tier 1 (Basic) | Tier 2 (Verified) | Tier 3 (Full) |
|---------|---------------|------------------|---------------|
| **Requirement** | Phone + BVN | Tier 1 + Gov ID | Tier 2 + Address proof |
| **Daily send limit** | ₦50,000 | ₦500,000 | ₦5,000,000 |
| **Single transaction** | ₦20,000 | ₦200,000 | ₦2,000,000 |
| **Balance limit** | ₦300,000 | ₦3,000,000 | Unlimited |
| **International transfers** | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Business features** | ❌ | Limited | Full |

## Tier 1 — Basic

Automatically granted on registration.

**Requirements:**
- Nigerian phone number (verified via OTP)
- BVN (Bank Verification Number)

**Verification time:** Instant

## Tier 2 — Verified

**Requirements:**
- Tier 1 verified
- One of: National ID, Voter's Card, Passport, Driver's Licence

**Verification time:** Usually within 2 hours, up to 24 hours

**How to upgrade:**
1. Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Identity Verification**
2. Select your document type
3. Upload a clear photo of the front and back
4. Submit

## Tier 3 — Full

**Requirements:**
- Tier 2 verified
- Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement — dated within 3 months)
- Selfie with document (liveness check)

**Verification time:** 1–3 business days (manual review)

## Verification Status

Your verification status is always visible in **Dashboard → Profile → Verification Status**:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `PENDING` | Under review |
| `APPROVED` | Verification successful |
| `REJECTED` | Verification failed — see reason |
| `REQUIRES_INFO` | More information needed |

<Callout type="info" title="Rejected submissions">
If your submission is rejected, the rejection reason will be displayed in the app. Common reasons include blurry images, expired documents, or name mismatch with BVN.
</Callout>

## Business Accounts

Business accounts have a separate KYC process (CAC registration, directors' IDs, etc.). See [Teams & Permissions](/docs/business/teams-permissions) for details.
