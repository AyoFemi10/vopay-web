## Transaction Limits

Limits apply per calendar day and reset at midnight WAT.

| Limit | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 |
|-------|--------|--------|--------|
| Daily send | ₦50,000 | ₦500,000 | ₦5,000,000 |
| Single transfer | ₦20,000 | ₦200,000 | ₦2,000,000 |
| Daily receive | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Wallet balance | ₦300,000 | ₦3,000,000 | Unlimited |
| International (USD) | — | — | $10,000/day |

## Transfer Fees

### Domestic Transfers (NGN)

| Amount | Fee |
|--------|-----|
| ₦0 – ₦5,000 | ₦10 |
| ₦5,001 – ₦50,000 | ₦25 |
| ₦50,001 – ₦500,000 | ₦50 |
| Above ₦500,000 | 0.01% (capped at ₦2,500) |

<Callout type="success" title="VOPayX-to-VOPayX transfers are free">
Transfers between VOPayX wallets are always free, regardless of amount or tier.
</Callout>

### International Transfers (USD/GBP/EUR)

| Type | Fee |
|------|-----|
| USD wire transfer | $5 flat + 1% |
| GBP SEPA | £3 flat + 0.8% |
| EUR SEPA | €3 flat + 0.8% |

### Currency Conversion

A 1.5% FX margin is applied on top of the mid-market rate when converting between currencies.

## Withdrawal Fees

| Destination | Fee |
|-------------|-----|
| Nigerian bank account | ₦50 |
| Same-bank transfer | ₦25 |
| International bank | $5 + 1% of amount |

## Receiving Payments

| Type | Fee |
|------|-----|
| Receive from VOPayX user | Free |
| Receive via payment link | 1.5% (capped at ₦10,000) |
| Receive via card (Visa/Mastercard) | 1.4% + ₦100 |

## Invoice Payment Fees

When a customer pays your invoice, VOPayX deducts a processing fee of **1.5%** (min ₦50, max ₦10,000) before crediting your wallet.

## Limits Reset

- Daily limits reset at **00:00 WAT** (UTC+1)
- Monthly limits (if applicable) reset on the **1st of each month**

<Callout type="warning" title="Pending transactions count against limits">
Pending outgoing transfers count against your daily limit until they are completed or failed.
</Callout>
