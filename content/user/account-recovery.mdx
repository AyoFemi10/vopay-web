## Forgot Password

### Via the App/Web

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login screen
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for a reset link (valid for 15 minutes)
4. Click the link and set a new password

### Requirements for new password

- At least 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

<Callout type="info" title="Didn't receive the email?">
Check your spam folder. The email comes from `noreply@vopay.com`. If still not received, wait 5 minutes and try again.
</Callout>

## Forgot Transaction PIN

Your 6-digit transaction PIN is separate from your login password.

1. Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Security → Reset PIN**
2. Complete identity verification (OTP to registered phone)
3. Enter and confirm your new PIN

<Callout type="warning" title="PIN is not recoverable">
For security, VOPayX cannot retrieve your existing PIN. You must reset it to create a new one.
</Callout>

## Lost Access to Phone Number

If you no longer have access to your registered phone number:

1. Contact support via **support@vopay.com** with subject "Phone Number Change Request"
2. Provide:
   - Full name as registered
   - Email address on the account
   - Last 4 digits of BVN
   - A clear photo of your government-issued ID
3. Our team will verify your identity and assist with the number update within **1–2 business days**

## Two-Factor Authentication Recovery

### If you lost your 2FA authenticator app

1. Use one of the 8 backup codes provided when you set up 2FA
2. If you don't have backup codes: contact support@vopay.com — manual identity verification required

### Disable 2FA (with old device)

1. Go to **Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**
2. Click **Disable 2FA**
3. Enter the current OTP from your authenticator app

## Account Locked After Failed Attempts

After 5 consecutive failed PIN or password attempts, your account is temporarily locked for **30 minutes**. After 30 minutes, try again.

If your account remains locked or was locked by our fraud detection system, contact support.

## Suspicious Activity

If you notice unauthorised transactions:

1. **Change your password immediately** via the app
2. Go to **Settings → Security → Active Sessions** and log out all sessions
3. Contact support@vopay.com with details of the unauthorised activity
4. File a dispute for each suspicious transaction

<Callout type="error" title="Dispute window">
Disputes for unauthorised transactions must be filed within **60 days** of the transaction date.
</Callout>
