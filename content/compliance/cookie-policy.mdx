## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you use it.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the platform to function and cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `vp_session` | Maintains your login session | Session |
| `vp_csrf` | Prevents cross-site request forgery | Session |
| `vp_device` | Device fingerprinting for security | 1 year |

### Functional Cookies

These remember your preferences and choices.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `vp_theme` | Stores your colour theme preference | 1 year |
| `vp_lang` | Stores your language preference | 1 year |
| `vp_currency` | Stores your default currency | 1 year |

### Analytics Cookies

Used to understand how users interact with the platform (aggregated, anonymised data only).

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| `_vp_analytics` | VOPayX internal | Page views and feature usage | 90 days |

We use **Plausible Analytics** (privacy-first, no personal data):

### Fraud Prevention Cookies

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `vp_fp` | Fraud signal detection | 6 months |
| `vp_risk` | Session risk scoring | Session |

## 4. Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Firefox**: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge**: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

### Cookie Preferences

For non-essential cookies, manage your preferences at:
**Dashboard → Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences**

<Callout type="warning" title="Disabling necessary cookies">
Disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from logging in and using the platform.
</Callout>
