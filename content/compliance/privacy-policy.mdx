## 1. Introduction

VOPayX Technology Limited ("VOPayX", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our platform and services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account information**: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth
- **Identity documents**: National ID, passport, driver's licence, BVN
- **Financial information**: Bank account details, transaction history
- **Business information**: CAC registration, business name, directors' details

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- Device information (model, OS, browser, IP address)
- Usage data (pages visited, features used, timestamps)
- Location data (with permission, for fraud prevention)
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- Identity verification providers (e.g. Smile Identity)
- Banking partners for account verification
- Credit reference agencies (with consent)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Providing our services | Contract performance |
| Identity verification (KYC) | Legal obligation |
| Fraud prevention and security | Legitimate interests |
| Regulatory compliance (AML) | Legal obligation |
| Customer support | Contract performance |
| Service improvements | Legitimate interests |
| Marketing communications | Consent |

## 4. Data Sharing

We share your data only as necessary:

- **Banking partners**: For transaction processing
- **Identity providers**: For KYC verification
- **Payment processors**: For payment facilitation
- **Regulators**: As required by law (CBN, EFCC, NFIU)
- **Service providers**: With appropriate data processing agreements

We do **not** sell your personal data.

## 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Transaction records | 7 years (regulatory requirement) |
| Account information | Duration of account + 7 years |
| KYC documents | 5 years after account closure |
| Marketing preferences | Until consent withdrawn |
| Support communications | 3 years |

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion (subject to regulatory requirements)
- **Portability**: Receive your data in machine-readable format
- **Objection**: Object to processing for direct marketing
- **Restriction**: Request restriction of processing

To exercise any right, contact: **privacy@vopay.com**

## 7. Security

We protect your data using:

- AES-256 encryption at rest
- TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- Multi-factor authentication
- Regular third-party security audits
- ISO 27001 certified infrastructure

## 8. Cookies

See our [Cookie Policy](/docs/compliance/cookie-policy) for details on how we use cookies.

## 9. Children's Privacy

VOPayX is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect.
