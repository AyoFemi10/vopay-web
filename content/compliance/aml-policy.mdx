*Last updated: January 1, 2025*

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Counter-Terrorism Financing (CTF) Policy establishes the framework under which VOPayX Technology Limited operates to prevent the misuse of its platform for financial crime. This policy applies to all customers, employees, and third parties.

## 2. Regulatory Framework

VOPayX operates in compliance with:

- Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) AML/CFT Regulations
- Nigerian Financial Intelligence Unit (NFIU) guidelines
- Financial Action Task Force (FATF) recommendations
- Money Laundering (Prevention and Prohibition) Act 2022

## 3. Customer Due Diligence (CDD)

### Standard CDD (All customers)

- Full name
- Date of birth
- Address
- BVN (Bank Verification Number)
- Phone number verification

### Enhanced Due Diligence (High-risk customers)

Applied to:
- Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs)
- Non-resident customers
- Customers with complex transaction patterns
- High-value accounts

Enhanced measures include: additional documentation, source of funds declaration, senior management approval.

## 4. Prohibited Activities

VOPayX will not provide services to:

- Individuals or entities on OFAC, UN, or EU sanctions lists
- Shell companies without identifiable beneficial owners
- Anonymous or fictitious customers
- Known money launderers or terrorist financiers

## 5. Transaction Monitoring

Our automated monitoring systems flag:

- Transactions inconsistent with customer profile
- Rapid movement of large sums
- Transactions involving high-risk jurisdictions
- Structuring (breaking up transactions to avoid reporting thresholds)
- Unusual patterns in frequency or amount

## 6. Suspicious Activity Reporting

When suspicious activity is detected:

1. Transaction may be placed on hold
2. Internal investigation conducted within 48 hours
3. Report filed with NFIU if warranted
4. Customers are **not** notified of SAR filings (tipping-off prohibition)

## 7. Record Keeping

All customer records and transaction data are retained for a minimum of **7 years** as required by regulation.

## 8. Staff Training

All VOPayX staff receive AML/CTF training:

- On joining
- Annually thereafter
- When regulations change materially
